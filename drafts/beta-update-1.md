# Beta Update #1

Subject: Dinner, Planned — What's New + What's Coming

---

Hi everyone,

Thanks for trying out Dinner, Planned and for the early feedback — it's already shaping the app. Here's what's changed since you signed up:

**New Features**

- **Edit your recipes.** You can now edit any recipe after importing or adding it. Hit the Edit button on the Manage page, or click into any recipe and edit from there. Fix typos, adjust ingredients, add your own notes — make each recipe yours.

- **Grocery checklist.** The grocery page now shows an aggregated shopping list grouped by store section (produce, meat, dairy, pantry). Check items off as you shop — it saves your progress. Hit Share to send the list to your Notes app, a text message, or wherever you need it. You can also **add your own items** (eggs, milk, bread, etc.) in the Extra Items section — they're shared with your partner and show up every time.

- **Click meals to see recipes.** On the "This Week" page, tap any meal to pull up the full recipe with ingredients and directions — handy when you're standing in the kitchen.

- **Better mobile experience.** Bigger touch targets, full-screen recipe views, and a layout that works better on your phone at the store or stove.

**Coming Soon**

- Better recipe importing from more sources
- More ways to organize and tag your recipes
- Monthly view to help spread meals across weeks

**Keep the Feedback Coming**

Your feedback is what drives what we build next. If you haven't already, please share your thoughts: [Feedback Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSccKMPljDnR1sl344cTQirYQY4Ps2iMDH5GCDYE775lIw82YA/viewform?usp=dialog)

Thanks for being part of this!
